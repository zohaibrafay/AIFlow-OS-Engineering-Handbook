# Chapter 3: Market Analysis

## Purpose

This chapter will analyze the workflow automation, orchestration, integration, and AI workflow markets to inform platform priorities.

## Learning Objectives

- Identify market categories that overlap with AIFlow OS.
- Understand buyer, developer, operator, and end-user needs.
- Distinguish workflow automation, integration platforms, AI builders, and orchestration engines.

## Scope

The chapter covers market categories, trends, user needs, and opportunity areas. It does not make revenue projections or unsupported competitive claims.

## Deliverables

- Market category map.
- User need analysis.
- Platform opportunity summary.
- Risks and constraints.

## Future Sections

- Category definitions.
- User segment analysis.
- Adoption drivers.
- Operational and compliance pressures.
- AI-native workflow trends.
