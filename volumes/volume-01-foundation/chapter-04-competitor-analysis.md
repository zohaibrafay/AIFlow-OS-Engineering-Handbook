# Chapter 4: Competitor Analysis

## Purpose

This chapter will compare relevant workflow, automation, AI builder, and orchestration systems to clarify AIFlow OS design priorities.

## Learning Objectives

- Compare major systems by capability, extensibility, runtime model, and target audience.
- Identify architectural patterns worth studying.
- Avoid copying product surfaces without understanding system trade-offs.

## Scope

The chapter covers products and platforms such as n8n, Zapier, Make, Flowise, Langflow, Temporal, and Airflow. It does not rank vendors as a purchasing guide.

## Deliverables

- Competitor category framework.
- Capability comparison model.
- Architectural observations.
- Differentiation opportunities.
