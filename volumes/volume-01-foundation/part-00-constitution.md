# Part 00: Constitution

## Purpose

This part connects Volume 1 to the repository constitution. It explains how mission, principles, documentation governance, security expectations, and definition of done apply to all foundation chapters.

## Learning Objectives

- Understand the governance model for foundation content.
- Apply constitution principles during chapter authoring.
- Identify when a foundation change requires roadmap, glossary, master context, or ADR updates.

## Scope

This part references project-level governance and explains how to apply it inside Volume 1. It does not replace [docs/CONSTITUTION.md](../../docs/CONSTITUTION.md).

## Deliverables

- Constitution alignment for Volume 1.
- Review expectations for foundation chapters.
- Criteria for moving from structure to full chapter content.
